Financial Market Malaysia

This page showcases a collection of interactive visualizations that provide in-depth insights into various aspects of the financial market in Malaysia. The visualizations highlight key financial data, including currency exchange rates, company financial performance (sales, profit, and market value), and the interest rate trends set by Bank Negara Malaysia. By using different types of charts, such as world maps, scatter plots, and line charts, the visualizations allow users to explore and analyze the data from different perspectives. These interactive tools aim to help users understand the dynamics of Malaysia's financial landscape, track historical changes, and identify patterns in economic indicators over time.

Currency Exchange Rates against MYR

This visualization displays a world map where each country is colored according to its currency exchange rate against the MYR over time.

Monthly Interest Rate for Bank Negara Malaysia

This line chart shows the monthly interest rate for Bank Negara Malaysia from 1997 to 2024.

This visualization is created by Chung Min Yi. The data sources are from: Malaysia's Largest Companies Dataset, Currency Exchange Rates from data.gov.my, and Monthly Interest Rate from data.gov.my.
