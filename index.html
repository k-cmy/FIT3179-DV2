Financial Market Malaysia: A Data-Driven Insight

This dashboard presents a comprehensive view of the Malaysian financial market through various visualizations. We explore key aspects such as monetary aggregates, interest rates, currency circulation, and exchange rates, providing you with an interactive experience to understand the trends and changes in Malaysia's economy.

Monetary Aggregates: M1, M2, M3

This area chart illustrates the flow of money in the economy, categorized by M1 (currency in circulation and demand deposits), M2 (including savings and fixed deposits), and M3 (broader money supply). This visualization shows how different forms of money supply have evolved over time, reflecting Malaysia's economic conditions and policy impacts.

Interest Rate Trends in Malaysia

The line charts below represent the historical interest rates set by Bank Negara Malaysia. The left chart highlights monthly changes in lending and deposit rates, while the right chart shows yearly trends. This helps track borrowing costs and saving incentives over time, which are crucial for understanding Malaysia's financial landscape.

Currency in Circulation by Denomination

This streamgraph visualizes the distribution of various currency denominations (notes and coins) in circulation each month. It provides insights into the demand for different denominations and how their usage changes over time, reflecting consumer behavior and economic trends.

Global Exchange Rates Against MYR

This choropleth map shows the exchange rates of the Malaysian Ringgit (MYR) against major world currencies. By visualizing the global performance of the MYR, we can better understand Malaysia's trade competitiveness and international economic positioning.
